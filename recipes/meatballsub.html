Meatball Sub Sandwich

Ingredients

Sauce

  • Crushed Tomatoes | 1 28 oz. can
  • Onion | 1 large
  • Garlic | 4 large cloves
  • Lemon Juice | 1 tbs
  • Basil | 5-6 leaves crushed

Meatballs

  • Ground Beef | 2 pounds
  • Progresso Italian Breadcrumbs | 1/2 cup
  • Garlic Powder | 1/2 tsp
  • Onion Powder | 1/2 tsp
  • Eggs | 2
  • Salt | 1/4 tsp
  • Pepper | 1/4 tsp
  • Milk | 1/4 cup

Sandwich

  • Italian Bread Loaf | 2 large
  • Sliced Parmesan Cheese | 1 cup
  • Butter | 1/4 cup

Tools

  • Large Baking Sheet
  • Large Mixing Bowl
  • Cast Iron Skillet
  • Knives (+cutting board)
  • Gloves for forming meatballs
  • Parchment Paper

Recipe

Makes 8 Servings

Sauce

  1. Pour crushed tomatoes directyly into cast iron skillet on medium.
  2. Cut onion in half and add it to tomatoes.
  3. Dice garlic and add it, the crushed basil, and lemon juice to the tomatoes.
  4. Simmer anywhere from 30 minutes to several hours.

Meatballs

  1. Lightly whisk two large eggs in a small bowl.
  2. In a separate small bowl, combine ½ cup breadcrumbs, ½ cup grated Parmesan, ½ tsp garlic powder, ½ tsp onion powder, ½ tsp Italian seasoning, ¼ tsp salt, and ¼ tsp pepper.
  3. Add the whisked egg, breadcrumb mixture, ¼ cup milk, and 2 lb. ground beef to a bowl. Wear the gloves and your hands to mix the ingredients together.
  4. Let the meatball mixture sit for five minutes to allow the breadcrumbs to absorb some of the moisture.
  5. Divide and shape the meatball mixture into about 32 meatballs, 2 Tbsp each
  6. To bake the meatballs, preheat the oven to 400ºF. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Place the meatballs on the baking sheet with about one inch between each.
  7. Bake the meatballs for about 15 minutes, or until lightly browned, then transfer to a pot of red sauce and simmer for an additional 5 minutes

Sandwich

  1. Cut Italian bread into 16 slices (or 8 sandwiches). Optional: Toast in oven for 5 minutes at 400
  2. Ladel out 3-4 meatballs and sauce per sandwich.
  3. Add parmesan cheese to each sandwich and serve.
