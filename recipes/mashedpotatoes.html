Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Yukon Gold Potatoes | 5-6
  • Garlic Cloves, peeled | 3-4
  • Sour Cream | 8 oz
  • Butter | 6 tablespoons
  • Grated Chedder Cheese | 6 tablespoons
  • Green Onions | 2-3 stalks
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Optional: Bacon | 2-3 slices

Tools

  • Large Pot
  • Small Pan
  • Large and small knives (+cutting board)
  • Immersion Blender
  • Large Mixing Bowl

Recipe

Makes 4 Servings

  1. Large dice potatoes, add potatoes to boiling water.
  2. Cut green onions at an slanted angle and put aside green tops.
  3. Small dice garlic. Add garlic and white onion bottoms to a hot buttered pan, put aside when browned
  4. Optional: Cook bacon to package instructions
  5. One potatoes are soft, in a mixing bowl, add the potatoes, sour cream, softened butter, 3/4 of the cheese, and salt and pepper to taste. Blend well.
  6. Plate potato mixture. Top with green onion tops, the remaining cheese, (and bacon if added).
