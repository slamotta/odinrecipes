Aloha Lemonade

Ingredients

  • Pineapple Juice | 8 oz
  • Apple Juice | 4 oz
  • Lemon Juice | 6 tablespoons
  • Lime Juice | 6 tablespoons
  • Spirulina Powder | 4 teaspoons
  • Ice | .25 pounds
  • Optional: Vodka or Tequila | desired amount (2-3 oz)
  • Optional: A small lemon or lime | 2 slices

Tools

  • Large Pitcher
  • Long spoon, immersion blender, or milk frother.
  • Glasses (2)

Recipe

Makes 2 Servings

  1. Cut lemon or lime into wedges.
  2. Mix all juices and spirulina (and alcohol if added) together with a spoon, immersion blender, or milk frother in a large pitcher with a cover.
  3. Pour over ice, add lemon or lime wedges and serve immediately.
